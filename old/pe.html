Curriculum

Five points of contact.

Barack Obama's campaign team knew that one phone call is never enough; you need to create five meaningful interactions with a voter to ensure they’ll pull the lever for you on election day. Now, staffers from that historic campaign have developed a series of lessons that transform students from mediocre volunteers into highly effective, campaign-ready citizens.

Strategy

Understand how successful campaigns use volunteers to swing voters. Learn why you speak to different voters at different times and what you need to get out of the conversation.

Persuasion

Our curriculum is informed by groundbreaking new political science research. We'll teach you how to use “deep canvassing” to structure a conversation that steers a voter towards your candidate.

Civics

Learn about the system you're trying to change. Athens teaches you the mechanics of our government. You'll learn how congress actually works (and how to get them to change their mind).
